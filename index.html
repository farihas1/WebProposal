Background

Youth project is an independent charity that provides support to young people who are experiencing homelessness, unemployment, disadvantage, alcohol and other drug issues. They also aid young people in learning and gaining employment, opening up pathways and breaking the cycle of disadvantage.

Youth Projects 2020 impact report details that they have been rapidly adjusting to the new health crisis of covid-19, stating that they are the "last outpost" as more people are continuing to relay on their service to provide them basic needs such as medical supplies, food and isolation.

Research

Youth Projects as a non-profit organisation has been able to support more than 200 people who are homeless and provided them 'crisis accommodation' while also giving covid-19 testing and giving out food packs.

Their current donation service are not concisely put together, having 4 different ways to donate to the organisation. My main goal is to make a centralised website where people only need to visit one place where they can chose how to help.
